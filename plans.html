① GRAMMAR · going to / present continuous
📘 PLANES A FUTURO · going to / present continuous

Hay dos formas de hablar del futuro: going to (planes e intenciones) y el present continuous (arreglos con día/hora). Repasa y escucha al equipo planear el próximo proyecto.

🎯 be going to (planes) am/is/are + going to + base
I'm going to study.
We're going to launch the app.
📅 present continuous (citas) am/is/are + verbo-ing + hora
I'm meeting the client at 3.
We're flying on Monday.
? / negative Are you going to start?
I'm not going to wait.
Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
⚠ Watch out no olvides be: I going to go
I'm going to go.
going to = plan · present cont. = cita fija

🎧 AUDIO 1 · PLANS FOR THE NEW PROJECT

Sofia y Marcos planean el próximo proyecto. Fíjate en "going to" para planes.

⏱ 0:43🗣 Sofia 🇬🇧 + Marcos 🇲🇽

01What are they going to build?

02What is Marcos going to do on the project?

03When are they going to have the first meeting?

🎤Speaking: Describe a plan for a project or trip. Use I'm going to… three times.

EMPIEZA ASÍWe're going to create a new app. I'm going to write the code, and…

Sofia: Marcos, we're going to start a new project next month. Are you ready?

Marcos: Yes! What are we going to build?

Sofia: We're going to create an app for a travel company. It's going to be big.

Marcos: Great. How are we going to organize the team?

Sofia: I'm going to lead the design, and you're going to write the code. We're going to hire two more developers.

Marcos: When are we going to start?

Sofia: We're going to have the first meeting on Monday. I'm going to send the plan tomorrow.

Marcos: Perfect. I'm going to read it this weekend.

🎧 AUDIO 3 · NEW YEAR GOALS

Sofia y Marcos hablan de sus metas. Fíjate en "going to" para intenciones y planes.

⏱ 0:45🗣 Sofia 🇬🇧 + Marcos 🇲🇽

01What is Marcos going to do to improve his English?

02What exam is he going to take?

03What is Sofia going to do?

🎤Speaking: What are three things you're going to do this year? Use I'm going to… and one I'm not going to…

EMPIEZA ASÍThis year I'm going to study English every day. I'm also going to…

Sofia: Marcos, it's a new year. What are you going to do differently?

Marcos: Good question! I'm going to improve my English. I'm going to study every day.

Sofia: That's great. Are you going to take an exam?

Marcos: Yes, I'm going to take the B1 exam in summer. And I'm not going to use the translator so much.

Sofia: Excellent. Anything else?

Marcos: I'm going to learn a new programming language too. What about you?

Sofia: I'm going to run a marathon, and I'm going to spend more time with my family.

Marcos: Those are great plans. Good luck!
